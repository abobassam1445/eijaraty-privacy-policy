How do I add a new rental contract?
From the home screen, tap the "+" button at the bottom, then enter the required contract information such as address, start and end dates, and amount.
Can I attach photos or documents to a contract?
Yes! When adding or editing a contract, you can attach one photo from camera or gallery, or one PDF file from your device.
How do I know which contracts are expiring soon?
On the home screen, you can identify contract status by the colored dot next to each contract: green dot for active contracts, blue dot for upcoming contracts, and red dot for expired contracts.
Can I export my data?
Export feature will be available in upcoming updates. Currently, you can backup via system services (iCloud or Google Drive) if enabled.
How do I change the theme from light to dark?
Go to Settings from the bottom menu, you'll find the "Theme" option where you can choose between Light, Dark, or System default.
What if I forgot to add tenant information?
You can edit the contract anytime by tapping on it from the main list, then tap the edit button (pencil icon) and add the missing information.
How do I delete a rental contract?
Open contract details by tapping on it, then tap the delete button (trash icon) at the top. You'll be asked to confirm before final deletion.
What does the star mean next to some contracts?
The star means the contract is starred/important. You can star important contracts by tapping the star icon to quickly access them from the "Starred" section.
